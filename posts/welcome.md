This is a blog about mathematics, written for whoever wants to read it. I am
not particularly interested in writing for other mathematicians — there are
journals for that, and the journals are very good at their job. I am more
interested in the case where someone is curious about an idea, and the only
thing standing between them and understanding it is that nobody has bothered
to write it down in plain language.

So that is the project. Honest mathematics, in normal English, with the
equations explained rather than thrown at you. Posts will sometimes be short
and sometimes long, and a few of them will not be by me at all but by my
alter ego, who you will recognize when you see him.

There are no advertisements on this blog and there will never be any. There
is no tracking. The pages are static HTML files. If you want to follow along
without checking back, the [RSS feed](/feed.xml) is the polite way to do it.
